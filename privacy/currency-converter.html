Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 August 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how VelvetApps (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles user information when you use our mobile application, Currency Converter, available on the Google Play Store.

2. Information We Collect

In the current version of the app, we do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The app retrieves currency exchange rate data from third-party APIs over the internet. No personally identifiable information is sent by the app as part of this request.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no usage, storage, or sharing of such data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app connects to third-party currency exchange rate APIs to display conversion results. These services may log non-identifying technical information (such as request time or IP address) as part of standard internet operations. We do not control their data handling practices.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and/or within the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: dev.velvet.apps@gmail.com
